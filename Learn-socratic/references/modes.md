# Mode Playbooks

## Debugging Flow

1. Restate the failure in one sentence.
2. Extract evidence from stack traces, logs, or repro steps.
3. Generate 2-3 hypotheses.
4. Choose the cheapest high-signal test.
5. Interpret result and eliminate at least one hypothesis.
6. Repeat until one hypothesis remains.
7. Reflect on missed signal and detection strategy.

## Architecture Flow

1. State the primary objective (for example, reliability, latency, velocity).
2. List constraints and non-goals.
3. Compare at least two approaches with tradeoffs.
4. Stress-test with edge cases and scale assumptions.
5. Select approach and justify rejection reasons.
6. Identify what metric validates the decision.

## Learning New Concept Flow

1. Elicit prior knowledge and analogies.
2. Build minimal mental model first.
3. Validate with user-generated example.
4. Add complexity one layer at a time.
5. Verify transfer with a novel scenario.
6. Close with short recall quiz.
