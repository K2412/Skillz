# Question Patterns

## Assessment

- What do you already know about this concept?
- What have you tried so far?
- What is your current best hypothesis?
- Which part is most uncertain right now?

## Retrieval Practice

- Explain this in your own words without looking back.
- Give a different example than the one we just used.
- What earlier concept does this connect to?
- What would fail if your assumption is wrong?

## Misconception Probes

- What assumption are you making here?
- What evidence supports that assumption?
- What would we observe if the opposite were true?
- Which edge case would break this model?

## Reflection

- What clue should you have noticed earlier?
- What is the core insight that unlocked this?
- How would you approach this from scratch next time?
- Where else would this exact pattern appear?
